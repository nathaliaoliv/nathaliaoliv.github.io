Dinner

Carne Cozida com Mandioca / Beef and Yuca Stew

Ingredients
2 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into about 6 pieces
1 clove garlic, finely grated
1/2 cup of diced yellow onion
Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
1 tesapoon of ground turmeric
1 teaspoon of garlic powder
2 teaspoon of soy sauce
vegetable oil
How to make it
Rub the meat with salt, pepper and soy sauce
Turn the Instant Pot to the Steam setting and add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
After heating the oil, add the onion and garlic and let it brown.
Switch the Instant Pot to the beef position and add the seasoned meat. Wait until it is done.
Open the instant pot and add the frozen cassava and two more cups of water mixed with the ground turmeric and garlic powder.
Set the Instant Pot to the vegetable setting and wait until it is done.
Open the instant pot and turn on the cooking mode for about 10 minutes to incorporate the liquid.
Inspired by:
Nathalia Oliveira
Ideas
Add diced cilantro on top of it
Pair it with white rice
